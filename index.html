The Artist

Raw emotion captured in charcoal and graphite.

There is a profound stillness in the act of sketching. For me, it is not merely about replicating what the eye sees, but translating what the heart feels into the stark contrast of black and white.

Using graphite and charcoal, I seek out the hidden narratives in everyday faces, the quiet dignity of age, and the raw power of nature.
